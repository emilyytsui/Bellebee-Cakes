About Our Cakes

All cakes will have 2 layers of chiffon cake and 1 layer of cream in between. Our chiffon cake is soft, fluffy, and moist. Made fresh to order from eggs, sugar, cake flour, baking powder, and oil. Cakes come in 4-inch and 6-inch sizes: 4-inch cakes yield 1-2 servings, while 6-inch cakes yield 5-6 servings.

Frosting Types


Swiss Meringue Buttercream (SMBC)

  • Ube Chiffon cake with Ube SMBC
  • A smooth type of frosting used to decorate or fill desserts
  • Ingredients: Egg whites, sugar, butter

SMBC is much smoother and looks more neat and put together. However, some might not enjoy the taste as much as a whole mini cake covered in whipped cream frosting. It's up to you :)


*Note: There is whipped cream frosting in between the two layers of cake. Only the outside of the cake can be covered with SMBC.

Whipped Cream Frosting (WCF)

  • Ube Chiffon cake with Ube WCF
  • A sort of bumpy type of frosting used to decorate or fill desserts
  • Ingredients: Heavy cream, sugar, lemon juice

WCF is more delicate and lighter than SMBC. It's perfect for those who prefer a less sweet option but may not have the same firmness as SMBC when decorating.
